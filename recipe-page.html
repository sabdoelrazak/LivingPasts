Traditional Hoerendrekjens

From the pages of 'De Utrechtse Keukenmeid", we visit the dubiously-titled dish named 'Hoerendrekjens'. Hoeredrekjens, also known as 'Hoerendreet' -- a very interesting name for a dish. 'Hoeren' translates to prostitutes, and 'drekje(n)s' and 'dreet' translate to 'fart' and 'excrement' respectively. Considering this peculiar and slightly insulting name for a dish, we wonder how appetizing a dish like this could be!
